## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I install the application?
See our [Documentation](documentation.md) for detailed installation instructions.

### The app won't connect to a meeting. What should I do?
1. Verify you have the correct meeting ID and password from Moodle
2. Check your internet connection
3. Ensure the meeting hasn't ended or been deleted
4. Try restarting the application

### Do I need a Zoom account?
No. This application allows you to join meetings anonymously without a Zoom account.

### Which operating systems are supported?
- Windows 10/11
- macOS 10.14+
- Linux (Ubuntu 20.04+)

### How do I uninstall the application?
See the "Uninstallation" section in our [Documentation](documentation.md).
