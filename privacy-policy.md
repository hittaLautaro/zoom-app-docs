# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 11, 2025

## Overview
Moodle Zoom Meeting Client is a desktop application that enables users to join Zoom meetings from Moodle LMS. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

## Data Collection
Our application does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:
- No user account information is collected
- No meeting credentials are stored permanently
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No personal information is retained after closing the application

## Temporary Data Usage
Meeting credentials (meeting ID and password) obtained from your Moodle system are:
- Used only in memory during active sessions
- Never written to disk or persistent storage
- Automatically cleared when the meeting ends or app closes

## Third-Party Services
This application connects to Zoom's services to facilitate meeting participation. Please review Zoom's privacy policy at https://zoom.us/privacy for information about Zoom's data practices.

## User Rights
Since we do not collect or store personal data, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete. Users maintain full control as no data persists beyond the active session.

## Children's Privacy
Our application does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for educational use under institutional supervision.

## Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
